Zach Sclare Hits Major Return Milestone

News / 25/10/2025 / 0 Likes

It was a hugely encouraging milestone for Zach Sclare at the weekend as he completed his first full ninety minutes in more than twelve months following a long-term injury.

The medical and conditioning staff have worked tirelessly with Zach during his rehabilitation, creating a comprehensive recovery schedule that allowed him to build his fitness back safely and effectively.

His return game was marked by an assist and an incredibly determined performance, highlighting his resilience and mental fortitude. We are delighted to see him back on the pitch and look forward to his continued progress this season.

Well done, Zach! Your hard work has paid off.
