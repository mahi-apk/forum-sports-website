Our Team

Meet the experts who integrate market intelligence and performance data to drive unparalleled client success.

James

Director / FIFA Licensed Agent

James Co-founded Forum Sports Management with a vision to integrate technology and deep data analytics into talent representation. With over 15 years in the game, he has overseen over €500M in transfers and specializes in high-stakes contract negotiation across the Premier League, La Liga, and Serie A, focusing on long-term athlete legacy.

Lewis

Director - Head of Recruitment & Data Analyst

Lewis leads Forum Sports Management’s bespoke recruitment and performance model, drawing on extensive experience working within the Premier League and Football League. He has held roles as a tactical analyst and coach in England and across Europe, giving him a detailed understanding of the demands placed on players at the highest levels of the game. Having worked closely with coaching staffs, analysts, and players in elite environments, Lewis brings a practical, performance focused perspective to player development and recruitment. His work centres on understanding how players fit within tactical systems, coaching philosophies, and long term club strategies. Lewis is driven by a commitment to helping players maximise their potential through informed guidance and an honest understanding of what clubs are truly looking for at each stage of a player’s career.
