Tómas Bent Magnússon Marks First Goal for Hearts in Convincing Win

News / 23/10/2025 / 0 Likes

It was a big moment for Tómas Bent Magnússon in yesterday’s convincing 4–0 home win for Heart of Midlothian, as he scored his first goal for the club since his summer transfer.

Tómas, who joined from KR Reykjavík, has impressed since moving to Scotland, quickly establishing himself as a versatile and important player in the squad. His goal was a beautiful finish from a tight angle, capping off a superb team performance.

His adaptation to the Scottish game has been rapid, a testament to his professionalism and the diligent work put in by the coaching staff. This goal is the first of many to come!
